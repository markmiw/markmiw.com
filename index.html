Fun Facts

Why did I want to be an Electrical Engineer?

I actually wanted to be a doctor because I always wanted to help people. However, in college, I realized I didn’t really like to practice medicine. So I tried out engineering and loved it! I also figured if I could invent something, I could help a larger amount of people than a doctor could. It’s all about the “area under the curve”.

What's my favorite food?

Fried Chicken! My favorite cuisine is Japanese food though.

Why did I move to LA?

I was originally from Hawaii. Living on an island for 20+ years of your life gets a little boring. I wanted to see what was out there! No regrets thus far!

If I could invent something, what would it be?

If I ever decided to create my own startup, I would make something dealing with renewable energy and sustainability.
